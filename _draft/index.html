What we do

Cooperation models

    Dedicated Development Team

    Choose a dedicated team of proficient engineers who will manage your project from start to finish. With our exclusive development team, you can rest assured knowing that your project is in capable hands, allowing you to focus on other priorities without disruptions.

  2. 2

    Team Extension Model

    Augment your existing IT team with one or more skilled software developers from Alpina Tech.
